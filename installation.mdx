The Personal Knowledge Base runs everywhere you do. You can access it instantly in any modern web browser without installing a thing, download a native desktop app for macOS or Windows for a faster, deeper experience, or keep your notes at your fingertips on iOS and Android. All three platforms share the same account and sync your notes in real time.

## Installation by Platform

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web">
    The web app is the fastest way to get started — no download required.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open your browser">
        Launch any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge) on your computer or tablet.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Go to the app URL">
        Navigate to **app.yourknowledgebase.com**. The app loads entirely in the browser — no extension or plugin is needed.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign in or create an account">
        Click **Sign In** if you already have an account, or click **Create a free account** to register with your email address. You can also continue as a guest to explore the app without signing up.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Bookmark the app">
        Press **Cmd+D** (macOS) or **Ctrl+D** (Windows/Linux) to bookmark the URL so you can return in one click. For an app-like experience on a desktop browser, use **Install app** from the browser menu to pin it as a Progressive Web App (PWA).
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Desktop (macOS & Windows)">
    The native desktop app offers faster startup, global keyboard shortcuts, and support for local-only storage without an internet connection.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Download the installer">
        Go to **yourknowledgebase.com/download** and click the button for your operating system:
        - **Download for Mac** — downloads a `.dmg` file
        - **Download for Windows** — downloads a `.exe` installer
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install the app">
        <Tabs>
          <Tab title="macOS">
            1. Open the downloaded `.dmg` file.
            2. Drag the **Personal KB** icon into your **Applications** folder.
            3. Eject the disk image.

            On first launch, macOS may display a security prompt. Click **Open** to confirm you trust the app.
          </Tab>
          <Tab title="Windows">
            1. Double-click the downloaded `.exe` installer.
            2. If Windows Defender SmartScreen appears, click **More info** → **Run anyway**.
            3. Follow the installation wizard and click **Install**.
          </Tab>
        </Tabs>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch and sign in">
        Open **Personal KB** from your Applications folder (macOS) or Start menu (Windows). Sign in with your account credentials, or create a new account directly from the app.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Mobile (iOS & Android)">
    Capture ideas on the go with the mobile app. It supports offline note-taking and syncs automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find the app in your store">
        - **iOS:** Open the **App Store** and search for `Personal Knowledge Base`.
        - **Android:** Open **Google Play** and search for `Personal Knowledge Base`.

        Look for the official app published by **Your Knowledge Base, Inc.** with the blue notebook icon.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install the app">
        Tap **Get** (iOS) or **Install** (Android) and wait for the download to complete. The app is free to install.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign in to your account">
        Open the app, tap **Sign In**, and enter your email and password. If you are new, tap **Create Account** to register. Your existing notes and notebooks will sync automatically once you are signed in.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Enable the **Share Sheet extension** during onboarding (iOS) or in your device settings (Android). This lets you clip text, links, and articles from any app directly into your knowledge base.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## System Requirements

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    - Google Chrome 100+
    - Mozilla Firefox 100+
    - Apple Safari 15+
    - Microsoft Edge 100+

    JavaScript must be enabled. Browser extensions that block scripts may interfere with the app.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Desktop" icon="laptop">
    - **macOS** 12 Monterey or later
    - **Windows** 10 (64-bit) or later
    - 200 MB free disk space
    - 4 GB RAM recommended
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mobile" icon="mobile">
    - **iOS** 15 or later (iPhone and iPad)
    - **Android** 10 or later
    - 100 MB free storage
    - Internet connection required for initial sign-in
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  All your notes sync automatically across every device where you are signed in to the same account. Changes you make on one device appear on all others within seconds.
</Note>

<Tip>
  The web app works offline in browsers that support Progressive Web Apps (Chrome and Edge on desktop). Any notes you create or edit while offline are saved locally and sync to your account as soon as you reconnect to the internet.
</Tip>
