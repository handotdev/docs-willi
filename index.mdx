## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install or open the app">
    Download the desktop app, install the mobile app, or open the web version in your browser. See [Installation](/installation) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first note">
    Click **New Note** and start writing. Use plain text, Markdown, or rich formatting — whatever suits you. See [Capturing Notes](/guides/capturing-notes).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize with notebooks and tags">
    Group related notes into [Notebooks](/concepts/notebooks) and label them with [Tags](/concepts/tags) so you can always find them later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search and retrieve">
    Use [Full-text Search](/concepts/search) to surface any note by keyword, tag, or notebook in milliseconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
