You can open Settings at any time by clicking the **gear icon** in the bottom-left sidebar or pressing <kbd>Cmd/Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>,</kbd>. From here you control everything about how your knowledge base looks, behaves, syncs, and protects your data — changes take effect across the entire app immediately.

## Settings Categories

<Accordion title="Account">
  Manage your personal profile and login credentials under **Settings → Account**.

  | Option | What It Does |
  |---|---|
  | Display Name | Sets the name shown in the app header and any shared notes |
  | Email Address | Updates the email used for login and notifications |
  | Password | Changes your account password; requires current password to confirm |
  | Profile Picture | Uploads or removes the avatar shown in collaborative views |
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Editor">
  Fine-tune the writing experience under **Settings → Editor**.

  | Option | What It Does |
  |---|---|
  | Default Font | Chooses the typeface used in the note editor |
  | Font Size | Sets the base text size (applies globally to all notes) |
  | Line Spacing | Adjusts vertical space between lines for readability |
  | Markdown Auto-Preview | Toggles live rendering of Markdown as you type |
  | Spell Check | Enables or disables the built-in spell checker |
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Sync">
  Control how and when your notes sync to the cloud under **Settings → Sync**.

  | Option | What It Does |
  |---|---|
  | Cloud Sync | Enables or disables syncing your notes to your cloud account |
  | Sync Frequency | Sets how often the app pushes changes (real-time, every 5 min, or manual) |
  | Conflict Resolution | Chooses whether to keep the local copy, the remote copy, or prompt you on conflict |
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Storage">
  Review and manage local data under **Settings → Storage**.

  | Option | What It Does |
  |---|---|
  | Local Storage Usage | Displays a breakdown of how much disk space your notes and attachments occupy |
  | Clear Cache | Removes temporary files to free up space without deleting any notes |
  | Data Directory (Desktop) | Changes the folder on your computer where notes are stored locally |
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Notifications">
  Stay on top of time-sensitive notes under **Settings → Notifications**.

  You can enable reminder notifications for any note that has a due date assigned. Toggle **Due-Date Reminders** on to receive an alert at the scheduled time, and choose your preferred lead time (e.g., 15 minutes, 1 hour, or 1 day before).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Privacy">
  Protect your data at rest under **Settings → Privacy**.

  Enabling **Note Encryption at Rest** encrypts the local copy of your notes using AES-256. You will be prompted to set an encryption passphrase the first time you turn this on. Keep your passphrase safe — encrypted notes cannot be recovered without it.
</Accordion>

<Note>
  Settings are saved immediately — there is no Save button.
</Note>
