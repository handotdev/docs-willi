The Personal Knowledge Base is a personal note-taking tool built around one idea: your thoughts deserve a reliable home. Whether you are jotting down a fleeting idea, structuring a research project, or linking concepts across dozens of notes, the app gives you a single, searchable space that grows with you. Think of it as a second brain — always available, always organized, and always yours.

## Why It Exists

Most people lose good ideas because they have nowhere consistent to put them. Sticky notes get lost, browser tabs pile up, and scattered files become impossible to search. The Personal Knowledge Base solves this by giving you a structured yet flexible environment where every thought is one search away.

The app is built around three core promises:

- **Capture** — Get ideas down quickly, in any format, before they slip away.
- **Organize** — Group related notes into notebooks, label them with tags, and connect them through links.
- **Retrieve** — Find anything instantly with full-text search, tag filters, and linked note graphs.

## Core Features

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Notes" icon="file-lines">
    Write in Markdown or rich text. Embed images, code blocks, and checklists. Every note is versioned so you can always roll back.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notebooks" icon="book">
    Group related notes into notebooks. Create as many as you need, nest them for deeper structure, and share them with collaborators.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tags" icon="tag">
    Apply multiple tags to a single note and filter your entire library by any combination of tags — no rigid folder hierarchy required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Search" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Full-text search across every note, notebook, and tag. Press <kbd>Cmd/Ctrl+K</kbd> from anywhere in the app to open the search palette instantly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Note Linking" icon="link">
    Reference any note from inside another using `[[Note Title]]` syntax. Build a connected web of knowledge and navigate it visually in the graph view.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Templates" icon="table-layout">
    Jump-start new notes with pre-built or custom templates for meeting notes, project plans, daily journals, and more.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
